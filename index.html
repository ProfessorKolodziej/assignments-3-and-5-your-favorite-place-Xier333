Miami

Located at the southern tip of the United States, in beautiful Florida, Miami enjoys warm weather year-round. The stunning beaches and clear waters attract people to participate in various water activities here.

Top attractions

I. Diving Spots

Miami is not only blessed with crystal clear waters, but also a rich underwater landscape. Both natural and human landscapes. Yes, there are human landscapes underwater as well, namely various shipwrecks, and there is even a statue of Christ and sunken tanks here! As well, these dive sites are very shallow and require very simple skills. Don't worry if you're new to diving, there are also many dive clubs that can help you have a lot of fun on your first dive.

II. Miami Beach

Miami Beach is famous for its long stretches of golden sand beaches and crystal-clear waters. The beach scenery here is captivating, with wide sandy shores perfect for sunbathing, beach volleyball, surfing, and other water activities. Transportation and amenities are also very convenient here, so you don't need to exert much effort to enjoy everything.

III. Miami Design District

The Miami Design District is an area filled with an artistic and design ambiance, boasting numerous galleries, art exhibitions, sculptures, and street art. It gathers internationally renowned designer brands and artists, providing visitors with the opportunity to admire and purchase unique artworks and design pieces. It is also a popular destination for fashionable shopping, with many boutique stores, fashion brands, and luxury shops.

IV. Vizcaya's Museum

The museum features an impressive collection of European art and furnishings, including paintings, sculptures, and decorative arts, meticulously curated to reflect the lavish lifestyle of the Gilded Age. Visitors can explore the beautifully preserved rooms of the main house, adorned with intricate details and ornate architecture. Beyond the mansion, Vizcaya's expansive gardens offer a tranquil retreat amidst lush greenery, elaborate fountains, and serene waterways.

V. Everglades National Park

This s a vast and unique wilderness area renowned for its diverse ecosystems and abundant wildlife. Encompassing over 1.5 million acres of wetlands, mangroves, and sawgrass marshes, it is the largest tropical wilderness of its kind in the United States. The park is home to a remarkable array of plant and animal species, including rare and endangered species such as the American crocodile, the Florida panther, and the West Indian manatee.

Don't Miss

Tips for Food

Coming to a coastal city, of course, you have to indulge in seafood! Whether it's fresh sashimi or seafood dishes like seafood pasta and grilled seafood, Miami is the perfect destination for seafood lovers.

  1. Make restaurant reservations in advance. Booking ahead ensures you'll enjoy piping hot dishes upon arrival.
  2. Be cautious of allergies. Seafood is delicious, but it can also potentially ruin your trip if you have allergies. So always be mindful of your allergens.
  3. Tip for Tipping In the US, a general advised tip for the restaurant servants is 18%.
  4. Parking Many restaurants have limited parking or valet parking only, make sure you check the parking situation before you go.
  5. Dress accordinly Make sure to read the dress code prior to your visit.
